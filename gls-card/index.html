Design

The new design focuses on 3 big aspects of nature: mountains, forests & oceans. Nature we treasure and make use of in various ways, but which due to our treatment is eroding, cut down and polluted. We should be reminded of their importance every day, especially when buying stuff.

Material

Bank cards are usually only valid for ~5 years, and discarded afterwards. It might seem a small factor, but at 2 or more cards per person it is an important detail for producing less waste.
