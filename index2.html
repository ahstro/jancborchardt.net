My current projects & activism is on the right. Jan-Christoph Borchardt is Design Dictator and free culture activist. He works on empowering people through improving the usability of open web technologies. Currently at the Unhosted movement, he previously was the original designer for ownCloud, the personal app platform, and started Libre Projects, the directory of open source web services. He is also involved with community events and founded Tel Aviv JS and StuttgartJS. I love to meet interesting people, which I mainly do through attending conferences and presenting at them. Such as CCC Berlin, Unlike Us Amsterdam, Students For Free Culture New York, International PHP conference Mainz. But what I find even better is small barcamps and user groups, some of which are JSZurich, MunichJS, HamburgJS. I also founded Tel Aviv JS and StuttgartJS. I also made an augmented reality concept with Hannes Fritz which was even featured in old media (Connect 12/2010) and planted some Dead Drops with free culture on it, which interested Prinz.
