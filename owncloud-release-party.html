ownCloud 6 release party

Part of the ownCloud team is in Munich and for the upcoming release of ownCloud 6 we’re organizing a meetup with some talks and hacking to celebrate the new version!
(There’s also a meetup in Berlin!)

Let’s meet this Wednesday, December 11, 7:00 PM
at Coworking Munich, Sonnenstraße 32, 80331 München
(U+Tram Sendlinger Tor)

We will have a short intro to ownCloud and what’s new for version 6. And since ownCloud is open source, after that we’ll show you how to contribute. So please bring your laptop! :)

Whether you are a designer or developer, come join in and make the open web better! Everyone is welcome! Bring your friends and spread the word.
